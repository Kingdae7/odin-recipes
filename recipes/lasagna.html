My Delicious Lasagna Recipe


Lasagna

Lasagna is a very delicious dish that dates back over 1500 years. Greeks and romans learned that combining a few simple ingredients and baking them together would end in an amazing explosion of flavor within your mouth.


Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian Sausage
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef season to your hearts content
  2. Boil Lasagna Noodles
  3. Layer Noodle, Meat, Cheese and continue to repeat
  4. Bake in dish
  5. Enjoy the dish
