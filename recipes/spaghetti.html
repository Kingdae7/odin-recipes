My Amazing Spaghetti Recipe


Spaghetti

Spaghetti and meatballs is an Italian-American dish created by immigrants between 1880 and 1920, not a traditional Italian meal. It originated from a need for resourceful cooking, combining affordable, plentiful American beef with Italian tomato sauce and pasta, allowing immigrants to enjoy larger portions of meat than in Italy.


Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Smoke kielbasa
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef season to your hearts content
  2. Boil Spaghetti Noodles
  3. Mix noodles, sauce and ground beef
  4. Enjoy the dish
